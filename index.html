Intro

This Web page is all about the very complicated statistics of Baseball.

Content

Baseball has many different stats that can be very complicated for the average fan to understand. There are so many different stats that make it easier to understand the sport of baseball and how good the players actually are. But these stats are way different to the ones that most are used to. Most people are familiar with the normal stats like Batting Average, Home Runs, and RBI but I perfer to look at some of the new advanced stats that are used very commonly today. I think that they give you a better look at players and how their regular stats may not be the whole picture and how players could be over/under performing. These stats show how sustainable a player's production really is.

Conclusion

I hope to make it easier for those who are new to the sport of baseball or are the average/casual fan to understand the complex stats of baseball that make the game so great.
